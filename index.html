Introduction

I am currently a PhD student at EDF R&D in collaboration with the CERMICS laboratory of École des Ponts ParisTech on the subject of model reduction for variational inequalities under the supervision of Alexandre Ern and Virginie Ehrlacher.

Research Topic

The work developed in this PhD is part of the model reduction framework for parametric variational inequalities.
